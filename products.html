About Us

China Zhenhua Electron Group Yuguang Electrical Ltd. Company is located in Guiyang National Hi-Tech Industrial Development Zone, covers an area of more than 83,000 square meters, existing staff of more than 1,200 people, including more than 300 senior technicians.

Zhenhua Yuguang is a professional company making vacuum interrupters, vacuum circuit breakers, high/low voltage switch assembly apparatus, as well as vacuum microwave device products at home, with the most advanced production, laboratory equipment and detection instrumentation.

After several decades of development, Zhenhua Yuguang has formed an annual production capacity of 300,000 pieces of vacuum interrupters, which are exported to the United States, Russia, Germany, Japan, India, South Korea, etc more than 20 countries and regions, and has the production, research and development technology of 120kA large current generator protection and 126kV high voltage grade vacuum interrupter, its production scale and R&D levels are among the best in China.

Zhenhua Yuguang Company has a sound management system, has passed the ISO9001:2000, ISO14001:2004 and OHSAS18001:2001 system certification; continuously awarded the “National Complying with Contract, Regarding Credit Unit” by National Industry and Commerce General Bureau for many years, continuously won the “Guizhou Province Famous Brand Product” title for 13 years; and owns more than 10 national patents. Zhenhua Yuguang is one of few companies applies “6 Sigma management system” in China. At the same time Zhenhua Yuguang also obtains “Adopting International Standard Product Symbol Certificate”, “Environmental Management System Authentication Certificate”, and “Professional Health and Safety Management System Authentication Certificate”.

Zhenhua Yuguang Company will continue to adhere to the credit management philosophy of “truth unchanged, service forever”, actively service for our customers, and make greater contributions to the development of power industry in the world.
